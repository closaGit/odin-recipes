Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a classic asian staple

INGREDIENTS

  • EGG
  • RICE
  • Soy SAUCE
  • some GREEN
  • some GARLIC

STEPS

  1. Use cold, leftover RICE for best results
  2. Put 1 tbps of cooking oil to your pan
  3. Wait until oil is hot, lookout for smoke
  4. Crack 2-3 eggs to the pan
  5. wait 1 minutes, then stir fry the eggs a bit
  6. Insert Rice to the pan
  7. Stir fry for about 2 minutes
  8. Insert GREEN, GARLIC, and SOY SAUCES
  9. Stir fry for another 2 minutes
  10. DONE
