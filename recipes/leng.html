leng recipe

Description

Leng is a chinese dish made from boiling boned-pork at low temperature for hours, then season the soup with chiili and spices

Ingredients

  • 2kg of boned-pork
  • a handful of chilli
  • 1,000ml of pork stock

Steps

  1. Put stock in boiling put
  2. put boned-pork
  3. Boil for 3-6 hours at low temperature
  4. put chilli and spices
  5. enjoy
